About

TowCalc is an offline-first towing calculator you can install on Android (PWA).

  • No account, no ads.
  • All data stored locally on your device.
  • Export/Import JSON for backups.
Disclaimer: For planning/education. Verify with door sticker, manuals, and scale weights.

Towing checklist

Use this checklist before towing to confirm your loaded setup is within all relevant limits.
On Results page:
  1. Payload within limits?
  2. Tongue weight within limits?
  3. Trailer weight (GVWR) within limits?
  4. Truck weight (GVWR) within limits?
  5. Truck & trailer combined weight (GCWR) within limits?
Other:
  1. Hitch Receiver within limits? Check hitch class rating.
  2. Hitch within limits? Check rating on hitch.
  3. Hitch ball within limits? Check rating on ball.
  4. Truck GAWR's within limits? Check axle loads on truck scale.
  5. Trailer GAWR within limits? Check axle loads on truck scale.
  6. Truck tires within load limit? Check tire weight rating.
