About

TowCalc is a lightweight, offline-first towing calculator you can install as an app on Android (PWA).

  • No account, no cloud, no ads.
  • All data stored locally on your device.
  • Export/Import JSON for backups.
Disclaimer: This tool is for planning and education. Always verify with your door sticker, owner’s manuals, and scale weights.
